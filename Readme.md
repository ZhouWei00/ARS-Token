## Tokenomics

### Distribution
- **Presale**: 20% (200,000,000 ARS)
- **Liquidity**: 20% (200,000,000 ARS)
- **Ecosystem & Rewards**: 20% (200,000,000 ARS)
- **Staking Rewards**: 15% (150,000,000 ARS)
- **Treasury**: 10% (100,000,000 ARS)
- **Marketing**: 8% (80,000,000 ARS)
- **Team**: 5% (50,000,000 ARS)
- **Advisor**: 2% (20,000,000 ARS)

### Tax Structure
- **Buy Tax**: 3% (1% Marketing, 1% Treasury, 1% Burn)
- **Sell Tax**: 5% (2% Marketing, 2% Treasury, 1% Burn)
- **Transfer Tax**: 0%
